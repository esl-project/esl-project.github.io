Initial Team

The people that started the ESL from scratch are the members of the Curating Team plus the following:

  • Alexandr Fonari, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta GA, US
  • Matteo Giantomassi, Université Catholoque de Louvain, Belgium
  • Georg Huhs, Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC), Spain
  • Ask Hjorth Larsen, Euskal Herriko Unibertsitatea/Universidad del País Vasco, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain
  • Stephan Mohr, CEA, Grenoble, France
  • Yann Pouillon, Euskal Herriko Unibertsitatea/Universidad del País Vasco, Donostia-San Sebastián, Spain

This initial effort was done within an extended workshop pushed and funded by CECAM, with its Director, Dominic Tildesley, and Mike Payne and Emilio Artacho as organisers.

Other Contributors

If you would like to contribute to the ESL, all you have to do is start editing. See this page for further information. We would also like to encourage you to contact us to let us know of your contribution, or if you have any questions or comments.
