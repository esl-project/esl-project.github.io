From ESL

Yann Pouillon is currently Researcher-Engineer at the University of the Basque Country, in San Sebastián, Spain. He is both a developer and user of scientific software, mainly focused on the simulation of matter at the atomic scale. He also puts his long experience in software engineering at the disposition of scientific communities, through his participation to various projects. Here are a few examples:

  • Abinit
  • ETSF_IO
  • Libpspio
  • LibXC
  • ESL Project Group on Launchpad
  • ETSF Project Group on Launchpad
  • Debian Nanoscale Physics Metapackage
  • Debian Nanoscale Physics Development Metapackage

You will find more details about Yann on the Nano-Bio Website and LinkedIn.
